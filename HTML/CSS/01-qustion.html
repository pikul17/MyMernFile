Dinosaur Evolution and History

Dinosaurs were a diverse group of reptiles that dominated Earth during the Mesozoic Era, and modern birds are their living descendants.

Dinosaurs were fascinating reptiles that lived millions of years ago during the Mesozoic Era.
They came in many shapes and sizes — some were small and swift, while others were enormous and powerful.
These creatures ruled the land for over 160 million years before becoming extinct, likely due to drastic
climate changes and a massive asteroid impact. Today, scientists study their fossils to understand how they lived,
what they ate, and how they evolved. Dinosaurs continue to capture our imagination, reminding us of Earth’s
ancient history and the incredible diversity of life that once existed.
